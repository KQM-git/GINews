I. New Character

<details open="true">
<summary><span style="color: rgb(204, 144, 70);"><span style="font-size: 0.1rem;">"Augur of Wonders" Linnea (Geo) </span></span><span style="font-size: 0.1rem;">(5-Star)</span></summary>

◇ Moon Wheel: Geo

◇ Weapon: Bow

◇ Naturalist and Advisor to the Nod-Krai Branch of the Adventurers' Guild.

◆ Linnea is a Geo support character who enables her party to trigger Lunar-Crystallize reactions. At the same time, she is also capable of providing Geo RES reduction, healing, and consistent off-field damage.

Tapping on Elemental Skill: Lumi attacks in Super Power Form, continuously dealing AoE Geo DMG to enemies. If there are Moondrifts nearby, she will additionally deal AoE Geo DMG to enemies, which is considered Lunar Crystallize reaction DMG.

Tapping on Elemental Skill or Normal Attack consecutively after using Elemental Skill: Lumi attacks in Ultimate Power Form, dealing to the enemy one powerful instance of AoE Geo DMG that will be regarded as Lunar-Crystallize DMG, before switching to Standard Power Form.

Elemental Burst: Linnea summons Lumi, which then attacks in Super Power Form and restores HP to the whole party and then continues to do so for all characters on the field. If Lumi is already on the field, this will refresh Lumi's on-field time, though it will not change the form Lumi is currently in.

While Lumi is on the field, the Geo RES of nearby enemies is decreased.

◇ Moonsign: Ascendant Gleam:

Linnea's Elemental Skill and Elemental Burst are further enhanced, and summoning Lumi to the field further decreases the Geo RES of nearby enemies.

◇ Outside of combat, Linnea's Charged Attack can change to a net attack that allows her to capture certain creatures. She can also collect ores by summoning Lumi to destroy forgeable ore deposits.

</details>

V. New Enemies

<details open="true">
<summary><span style="font-size: 0.1rem;">Watcher: Fallen Vigil</span></summary>

◇ Once a Watcher dwelling in the heavens, it can monitor and counter elemental attacks.

In combat, Watcher: Fallen Vigil will enter the Supreme Overseer state. While in this state, Watcher: Fallen Vigil will track the number of Pyro/Hydro/Electro/Cryo DMG instances it takes, respectively. When it has taken 3 instances of DMG from any of these elements, it will exit this state and record this element as a monitored target. If no element is monitored successfully, the record stays empty. For elements that are already being monitored, more instances of that elemental DMG must be dealt before it can be monitored again. After completing 2 instances of elemental monitoring, the Watcher: Fallen Vigil will prepare to unleash Phenomena's Delirium, releasing the previously monitored elements and summoning two corresponding Active Elemental Stars. If the element record is empty, it will instead summon Inert Elemental Stars, which are difficult to destroy. Break all Elemental Stars to interrupt the preparation of Phenomena's Delirium.

Located at Windrest Peak.

</details>

<details open="true">
<summary><span style="font-size: 0.1rem;">Domain Keeper</span></summary>

◇ A shadow construct born from the overflow of the mighty primordial being, it can monitor and counter elemental attacks.

In combat, the Realm Keeper will enter the Deluded Eye state. While in this state, the Realm Keeper will track the number of Pyro/Hydro/Electro/Cryo DMG instances it takes, respectively. When it has taken 3 instances of DMG from any of these elements, the Realm Keeper will exit this state and transform into the corresponding elemental form, applying that element to itself and gaining a RES increase in the corresponding element.

</details>
