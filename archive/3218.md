**〓Eligibility〓**

Adventure Rank 30 or above

Complete the Archon Quest "Ritou Escape Plan"

**〓Event Details〓**

● There are a total of six challenges in "Hyakunin Ikki," and a new challenge will be unlocked each day for the first six days of the event.

You can use the trial characters provided or your own characters to form 6 tag teams and take part in the challenge.

Each tag team can have up to two characters and two Secret Arts. Secret Arts can provide buffs to the team on the field.

※ The event challenges are not available in Co-Op Mode.
