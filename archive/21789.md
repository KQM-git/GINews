VII. Elemental Reaction

<details open="true">
<summary><span style="font-size: 0.1rem;">Stellar-Conduct Reaction</span></summary>

When certain conditions are met, Cryo and Electro can trigger Stellar-Conduct. A Starlight Prism will appear on the field, turning the surrounding area into a Polestar Field for a short period of time.

While within a Polestar Field: All characters receive a Cryo and Electro DMG Bonus, and when opponents are hit by Cryo or Electro attacks, the field will store the dissipating elemental energies and then completely release them every 4s. This further increases the aforementioned DMG Bonus and also increases the Base Stellar-Conduct DMG coefficient for 4s. Additionally, opponents will also have their Physical RES lowered.

Certain characters gain additional effects of "Radiance: Stellar-Conduct" while within the Polestar Field, enhancing certain Talents and skill effects.

Certain characters can utilize their Talents to deal Stellar-Conduct DMG to enemies. This DMG can trigger CRIT Hits.

</details>
