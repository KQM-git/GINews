**〓Event Duration〓**

2023/11/09 10:00:00 (server) – 2023/11/20 03:59:59 (server)

**〓Eligibility〓**

Adventure Rank 20 or above

**〓Event Details〓**

● "Misty Dungeon: Realm of Water" has a total of seven trials with different themes. Starting with the first day of the event, a new trial will be unlocked every day.

● During each trial, Travelers must complete the Final Challenge within the allotted time to break through the current challenge. The opponents that appear in the Final Challenge will be weakened in accordance with the number of Ancient Runes activated. The Final Challenge can be directly activated during the challenge.

● The character lineup you can select for each trial is different, and you can use the Trial Characters or your own characters to take part in the trials.

● If you have the trial character, the said trial character in the trial will adopt the Constellation levels of the character you actually own.
