# Version Details - What's New 
## Update Summary
![Banner](https://sdk.hoyoverse.com/upload/announcement/2020/11/11/0c4d0c742dde8334be30352fa3f5fb5b_4067277611421326976.jpg)

Dear Travelers:

To ensure that all Travelers have the best-possible Genshin Impact experience, our developers continually make optimizations and bug fixes to the game based on Travelers' feedback.

〓Known Issues〓

There is an issue whereby Yelan's model will display abnormally when wearing the "Tranquil Banquet" outfit and selecting "Leisure Time" or "Alpha Pose" in "Take Photo > Pose." This issue will be fixed in a future update.

〓<t class="t_gl" contenteditable="false">2025/08/08 11:10</t> Update Details〓

Fixes an issue under certain circumstances whereby when challenging the opponent "The Last Survivor of Tenochtzitoc" or "Battle-Hardened Pipilpan Idol," destroying the hat that its true form hides in did not deal DMG to it.

〓<t class="t_gl" contenteditable="false">2025/08/07 16:20</t> Update Details〓

- Fixes an issue whereby certain languages do not display subtitles during cutscenes in the Archon Quest "A Space and Time for You" and the Story Quest "Whispers Betwixt Stars and Night."

- Fixes an issue whereby solving certain puzzles in Easybreeze Holiday Resort unlocks the exploration achievement "My Heart Is Now Complete" in the Quahuacan Cliff area incorrectly. Travelers who have already unlocked the achievement through this issue will retain the achievement's completion status.

- Fixes an issue whereby a puzzle involving a Monetoo on the western coast of Wavey Bay in Easybreeze Holiday Resort cannot be completed as intended.

- Fixes an issue whereby the "Romantic Sunset Performance" gameplay mode on the southeastern side of Tete Isle in Easybreeze Holiday Resort cannot be activated as intended.

- Fixes an issue whereby the guidance icon for the objective "Talk to Pucli" in the World Quest "The Last Survivor of Tenochtzitoc" incorrectly appears in Liyue under certain circumstances.

- Fixes an issue whereby some stains in the time trial challenge "Master Sweeper" near the northeastern Waypoint of Tete Isle in Easybreeze Holiday Resort are abnormally obscured under certain circumstances.

- Fixes an issue whereby the bow effect of "Tamoto," the automaton doll summoned by Chiori's Elemental Skill, has an abnormal duration after the Version 5.8 update.

- Fixes an issue in Genius Invokation TCG where the Character Card "Mavuika" gains 2 Nightsoul points instead of 1 upon entering Nightsoul's Blessing after using her Elemental Burst outside of that state.

Travelers affected by the above issues can log out via the Paimon Menu and close the game client, then log in again to continue the game as normal.

〓<t class="t_gl" contenteditable="false">2025/08/01 20:00</t> Update Details〓

Fixes an issue whereby when carrying out the Story Quest "The World Is Your Canvas!," there is a small chance that the gameplay related to the quest objective "Operate the Dance-Off Stage and complete the performance" would not be enabled, resulting in the quest being unable to continue.

Travelers who encounter this issue can exit the game via the Paimon Menu, end the game client, and log in again to resolve the issue.

〓<t class="t_gl" contenteditable="false">2025/08/01 11:10</t> Update Details〓

Fixes an issue whereby the "Mora Chest" rewards did not appear after completing the graffiti mini-game at "Wavey Bay" in Easybreeze Holiday Resort.

Travelers who encountered this issue before the update can return to the gameplay area to obtain the rewards as normal.

〓<t class="t_gl" contenteditable="false">2025/07/31 21:00</t> Update Details〓

Fixes an issue whereby when carrying out the Story Quest "To Colorfall Cliffs!," there is a small chance that the quest objective "Return to the 'Airborne Asha'" could not be completed and quest progress would be rolled back abnormally.

Travelers who encounter this issue can exit the game via the Paimon Menu, end the game client, and log in again to resolve the issue.
