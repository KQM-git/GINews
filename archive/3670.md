# Event Wish "Epitome Invocation" - Boosted Drop Rate for Song of Broken Pines (Claymore) and Lost Prayer to the Sacred Winds (Catalyst)!
## Event Wish - Epitome Invocation
![Banner](https://sdk.hoyoverse.com/upload/ann/2023/06/20/54d6ba585cbc6e7f0114348a2f7392e7_8737207647955510312.jpg)

Travelers, stock up on weapons and characters in "Epitome Invocation" to make your party stronger in combat!

**〓Event Wish Details〓**

**Event Wish Duration** | **Promotional Weapons (5-Star)** | **Featured Weapons (4-Star)**
--- | --- | ---
After the Version 3.8 update — 2023/07/25 17:59:00 (server) | Song of Broken Pines (Claymore)  Lost Prayer to the Sacred Winds (Catalyst) | The Alley Flash (Sword)
^ | ^ | Alley Hunter (Bow)
^ | ^ | Rainslasher (Claymore)
^ | ^ | Favonius Lance (Polearm)
^ | ^ | Eye of Perception (Catalyst)

● During the event wish, the event-exclusive 5-star weapon Song of Broken Pines (Claymore) and the 5-star weapon Lost Prayer to the Sacred Winds (Catalyst) will receive a huge drop-rate boost!

● During the event wish, the event-exclusive 4-star weapons The Alley Flash (Sword) and Alley Hunter (Bow), as well as the 4-star weapons Rainslasher (Claymore), Favonius Lance (Polearm), and Eye of Perception (Catalyst) will receive a huge drop-rate boost!

● During the event wish, use Epitomized Path to chart a course towards a promotional 5-star weapon, such as Song of Broken Pines (Claymore) or Lost Prayer to the Sacred Winds (Catalyst). For more information on Epitomized Path, go to the Wish screen and select Details in the bottom-left corner.

※ Of the above weapons, the event-exclusive weapons will not be available in the standard wish "Wanderlust Invocation."
