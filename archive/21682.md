# Version Details - What's New 
## Update Summary
![Banner](https://sdk.hoyoverse.com/upload/announcement/2020/11/11/0c4d0c742dde8334be30352fa3f5fb5b_4067277611421326976.jpg)

Dear Travelers:

To ensure that all Travelers have the best-possible Genshin Impact experience, our developers continually make optimizations and bug fixes to the game based on Travelers' feedback.

〓Known Issues〓

Currently, under specific circumstances, when using voice features in Miliastra Wonderland on mobile or console devices, there is a chance that game audio may abnormally stutter or cut out. This issue will be fixed in a subsequent update.

〓<t class="t_gl" contenteditable="false">2026/04/10 11:15</t> Update Details〓

- Fixes an issue whereby, under certain circumstances, when approaching certain areas in the Temple of Space and Luyang Academy, the in-game camera position would become abnormal and characters would be unable to teleport.

Travelers affected by this issue can select the "Leave" button in the upper-right corner of the screen and select "Leave for Now." Then log out via the Paimon Menu by selecting "Exit to Login Interface" and log in again to resolve the issue.

- Fixes an issue whereby, under certain circumstances, certain chest(s) would disappear abnormally in the "Shooting Practice" challenge given by the NPC "Herstal" at Dornman Port.

Travelers affected by this issue can log out via the Paimon Menu by selecting "Exit to Login Interface" and close the game, then log in again. Head to the challenge area afterward and the corresponding chest(s) will appear automatically.

- To ensure a better gameplay experience for social deduction stages, the "Stranger Messages" feature in Miliastra Wonderland has been adjusted: While playing Miliastra Wonderland stages, messages from strangers will be temporarily hidden.

The development team will continue to monitor this feature and further optimize it in future versions.

〓<t class="t_gl" contenteditable="false">2026/04/09 18:35</t> Update Details〓

Fixes an issue in the Temple of Space whereby enemy drops would be abnormal in a specific location.

〓<t class="t_gl" contenteditable="false">2026/04/08 21:50</t> Update Details〓

Fixes an issue whereby, under certain circumstances, after returning to Teyvat from the Personal Lobby in Miliastra Wonderland, there was a chance that some in-game scenes would appear abnormal.

〓Version Update Bug Fixes〓

Fixes an issue whereby, under certain circumstances, when the Deepdark Shield of "Fisher of Hidden Depths" or "Lord of the Hidden Depths: Whisperer of Nightmares" took damage, there was a small chance that the DMG value would appear abnormal.
