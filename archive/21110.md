〓Eligibility〓

Adventure Rank 20 or above

Complete Archon Quest Prologue: Act III "Song of the Dragon and Freedom"

Unlock any Statue of The Seven in Fontaine

※ Complete the Archon Quest "Incandescent Ode of Resurrection," the Story Quests "Animula Choragi Chapter: Act I" and "Dulciaria Structura Chapter: Act I," Tribal Chronicles "Mushroom Realm Mystery: Act III," as well as World Quest "The Long-Failed 'Graph Adversarial Technology'..." first to get the best experience from this event.

〓Event Details〓

● During the event, Travelers will manage the Chevalmarin Film Fantasyland, a film-themed amusement park. The park features three main attractions: Eclectic Escapades, Movie Masterpieces, and Mellow Manor.

● By upgrading and expanding your attractions, recruiting staff, and choosing theme dishes, you can continuously enhance the Chevalmarin Film Fantasyland's service quality, leading to higher revenue.

● When you reach a designated amount of accumulated revenue, you can obtain Sequence of Solitude (Bow), Primogems, Crown of Insight, event-exclusive Weapon Refinement Materials, and other rewards.
