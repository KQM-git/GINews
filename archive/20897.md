〓Eligibility〓

Adventure Rank 30 or above

〓Event Details〓

● From the first day of the event, you will be able to unlock the corresponding challenge every 2 days. You can speak to Helka and engage in challenges of different difficulties.

● In Fearless difficulty, you can also adjust the number of selected Special Topics provided by Helka. More Special Topics selected will make for a more difficult challenge.

● Complete challenge objectives at different difficulties to obtain the corresponding ratings and rewards.

● A maximum of two players can carry out the challenge in Co-Op Mode.

● Only challenge records achieved in Single-Player Mode can be reviewed or exhibited.
