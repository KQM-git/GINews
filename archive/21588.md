〓Event Details〓

● During the event, four gameplay modes will be available: "Echoes of Memory," "A Chat Over Fine Drinks," "Lionfang's Radiant Onslaught," and "Favonius Falcon's Recon Log." Complete these modes to obtain Windscape Splendor, which can be used to unlock the corresponding rewards.

● In "Echoes of Memory," some things differ from how they appear in the real world. You must use the Spotter Kamera to capture these discrepancies and restore them.

● In "A Chat Over Fine Drinks," you will take on the role of mixologist at the Knights of Favonius' drink stand. Prepare drinks based on each guest's requirements, and seek inspiration for improvements through casual conversations to create one-of-a-kind specialty cocktails.

● In "Lionfang's Radiant Onslaught," you will participate in a battle-based stage play, defeating enemies to accumulate Aegis. Use these points to summon the Shadow of the Lionfang Knight for support and dominate the battlefield with Sword Pressure.

● In "Favonius Falcon's Recon Log," you and Paimon command a Recon Falcon, flying along designated routes to scout and take down simulated enemy targets.

● Complete the event's various challenges to obtain Windscape Splendor. Reach a certain amount of Windscape Splendor to obtain rewards such as the weapon appearance "Phantasmal Gift: Star Omen," Primogems, and Crown of Insight.

※ The weapon appearance "Phantasmal Gift: Star Omen" obtained during the event will remain available for use after the event ends.
