Complete the corresponding Archon Quest within the specified duration to obtain an additional reward of Primogems ×500.

After the Version 5.3 update – <t class="t_lc" contenteditable="false">2025/02/11 14:59</t>

Adventure Rank 28 or above

Complete Archon Quest Chapter V: Interlude "All Fires Fuel the Flame"
