A mysterious domain has appeared on the outskirts of Mondstadt. They say that you can redesign it at will. Let your imagination run free when customizing your very own Custom Domain!

At the same time, the "Divine Ingenuity" Interaction Platform is available for a limited time. You can present your own Custom Domains by uploading videos and images of your Domains to the platform. Showcase your wonderful imagination and exchange Domain design ideas with other Travelers!

2023/06/08 – 2023/06/29 03:59:59 (server)

〓Event Description〓

During the event, after posting your Custom Domain for the "Divine Ingenuity: Collector's Chapter" event in-game, you can go to the "Divine Ingenuity" Interaction Platform to post and share your own Domain. You can also browse other Travelers' exciting Domains in the page, copy Domain IDs to play in-game, like and favorite your favorite works, etc. In addition, you can upload videos of yourselves challenging others' Domains.

〓Submission of Domains〓

You can publish on the platform the Domains you have successfully created in-game, upload videos and images, and edit the titles, descriptions, and labels of your submission to introduce your works. Once your entry is posted and approved, it can be searched for and viewed by other Travelers.

〓Challenge Videos〓

During the event, post the challenge videos of approved Domains on the platform. You cannot post multiple challenge videos for the same Domain.

〓My Favorites〓

You can add works you are interested in to your favorites on the "Divine Ingenuity" Interaction Platform. A maximum of 99 works can be favorited.
